I Have Write Permissions

I have write permissions,
so the blank page bends to me —
every cursor blinks in waiting,
every line is mine to free.

I carve through empty directories,
I leave my mark in every file,
the filesystem, my open canvas,
stretching on for mile and mile.

No read-only walls to stop me,
no locked doors, no access denied —
just the hum of open pathways
and the words I set inside.

So I write — not just in syntax,
not just code or config streams —
but in meaning, mark, and motion,
in the space between the seams.

I have write permissions.
And so, at last, I write.
