## PART 6: BUYER PROGRAMS

### MSHDA MI 10K DPA Loan — BEST PROGRAM IN THE MIDWEST
- **$10,000 zero-interest deferred second mortgage** for down payment AND closing costs
- No monthly payments — repaid only when home is sold/refinanced
- Available in 236+ eligible MI ZIP codes. Most SE Michigan suburbs included.
- Requires MI Home Loan (MSHDA 30-yr fixed) as first mortgage
- Income limits: ~$95k–$115k for SE Michigan households
- https://www.michigan.gov/mshda

### Other Programs
