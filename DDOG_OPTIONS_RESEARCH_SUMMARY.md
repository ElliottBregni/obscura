## RISK MANAGEMENT FRAMEWORK

### Position Sizing Rules
1. **Maximum Risk Per Trade:** 1-2% of total portfolio
2. **Example:** $50,000 portfolio → max $500-1,000 risk per trade
3. **For $300 debit spread:** Max 1-3 contracts

### Liquidity Checklist
Before entering, verify:
- ✅ Volume > 100 contracts/day
- ✅ Open Interest > 500
- ✅ Bid-ask spread < 10% of option price

### Exit Strategy
**Profit Targets:**
- Credit spreads: Close at 50% max profit
- Debit spreads: Close at 75-100% max profit

**Stop Losses:**
- Debit spreads: Exit at 50% loss OR 7 days before expiration
- Credit spreads: Exit at 2x credit received OR technical level breach

### Earnings Management
- **Strategy 1:** Exit all positions 1-2 days before earnings
- **Strategy 2:** Trade AFTER earnings when IV has declined
- **Strategy 3:** Play earnings volatility with straddles/strangles (advanced)
